### Definition
A coupon exercise performed lying on your back. Press the coupon from chest level to full arm extension above you. The coupon equivalent of a bench press.

### How it's done
Lie on your back, hold coupon on your chest with both hands. Press straight up until arms are extended. Lower with control. On cadence or OYO.
