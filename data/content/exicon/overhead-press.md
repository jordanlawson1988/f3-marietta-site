### Definition
A shoulder exercise pressing a coupon from shoulder height to full extension overhead. One of the core coupon movements in F3.

### How it's done
Hold the coupon at shoulder height with both hands. Press straight up until arms are fully extended. Lower with control back to shoulders. On cadence or OYO.
