### Definition
A tricep exercise performed lying on your back. Hold the coupon above your chest with arms extended, bend at the elbows to lower the coupon toward your forehead, then extend back up.

### How it's done
Lie on your back, coupon extended above chest. Bend elbows to lower the coupon toward your head (hence the name), then press back up. Elbows stay pointed at the sky. On cadence or OYO.
