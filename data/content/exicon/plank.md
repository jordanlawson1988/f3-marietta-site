### Definition
An isometric core hold in either high (arms extended) or low (on forearms) position. Body forms a straight line from head to heels.

### How it's done
The Q calls "Plank it up" and PAX hold the position. Often used as a rest between exercises (spoiler: it's not rest) or while waiting for the six.

### Variations
- High Plank
- Low Plank (forearms)
- Side Plank
- Plank Jacks
- Shoulder Tap Plank
