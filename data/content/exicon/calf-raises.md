### Definition
Rise up onto the balls of your feet by contracting the calves, then lower back down. Simple but effective, especially off a curb or step for extra range.

### How it's done
Stand with feet shoulder-width apart. Rise up onto toes, hold briefly at the top, lower with control. On cadence. Often done off a curb for a deeper stretch at the bottom.
