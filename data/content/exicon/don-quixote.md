### Definition
Arm circles — small to large, forward and reverse. A warm-up staple named after the windmill-tilting knight. Almost always part of the Warm-a-rama.

### How it's done
Arms extended straight out to the sides. Circle forward in small circles, gradually increasing size. Reverse direction. On cadence. The Q typically calls "and reverse" to switch.
