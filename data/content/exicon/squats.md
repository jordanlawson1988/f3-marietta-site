### Definition
A fundamental lower-body exercise. Feet shoulder-width apart, send hips back and down until thighs are parallel to the ground, then stand.

### How it's done
On the Q's count in cadence. Down on 1, up on 2, down on 3, up on 4. Keep chest up, weight in heels, knees tracking over toes.

### Variations
- Jump Squats
- Sumo Squats
- Squat Pulses
- Prisoner Squats (hands behind head)
- Goblet Squats (with coupon)
