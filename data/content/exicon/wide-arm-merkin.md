### Definition
A merkin (push-up) with hands placed wider than shoulder width. Shifts emphasis to the chest and front deltoids.

### How it's done
Start in plank with hands placed 4–6 inches wider than shoulders on each side. Lower chest to the ground and press back up. On cadence or OYO.
