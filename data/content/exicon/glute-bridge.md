### Definition
Lying on your back with knees bent, drive hips toward the sky by squeezing glutes. A posterior-chain exercise that's deceptively hard at high reps.

### How it's done
Lie on back, feet flat on ground, knees bent. Press through heels and squeeze glutes to lift hips until body forms a straight line from shoulders to knees. Hold at the top, lower with control. On cadence or OYO.
