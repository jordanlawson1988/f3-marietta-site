### Definition
A squat with a wider-than-shoulder stance and toes pointed outward. Targets the inner thighs and glutes more than a standard squat.

### How it's done
Stand with feet well beyond shoulder width, toes angled out at 45 degrees. Squat down keeping chest up and knees tracking over toes. On cadence.
