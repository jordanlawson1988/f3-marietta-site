### Definition
A lower-body exercise where you step forward and drop the back knee toward the ground, then drive back up. A staple of nearly every Beatdown.

### How it's done
Step forward with one leg, lower until both knees are at 90 degrees. Front knee stays over the ankle. Drive through the front heel to return or step the back foot forward for walking lunges.

### Variations
- Walking Lunges
- Reverse Lunges
- Jumping Lunges
- Coupon Lunges (overhead or at chest)
- Side Lunges
