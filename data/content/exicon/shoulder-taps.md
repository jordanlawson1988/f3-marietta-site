### Definition
From a high plank position, alternate tapping each hand to the opposite shoulder while keeping hips as still as possible. An anti-rotation core exercise.

### How it's done
Start in high plank, feet shoulder-width for stability. Lift right hand and tap left shoulder. Return, then left hand taps right shoulder. That's one rep. On cadence or OYO.
