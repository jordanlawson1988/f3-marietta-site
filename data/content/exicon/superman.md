### Definition
A prone exercise where you simultaneously lift arms and legs off the ground, engaging the lower back and glutes. Looks like Superman flying.

### How it's done
Lie face down with arms extended overhead. Lift arms, chest, and legs off the ground simultaneously. Hold for a count, then lower. On cadence or OYO.
