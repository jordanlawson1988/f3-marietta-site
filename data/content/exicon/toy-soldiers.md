### Definition
A walking or stationary exercise where you kick one leg straight up while reaching the opposite hand toward the toe. Stretches the hamstrings and warms up the posterior chain.

### How it's done
Walk forward, kicking the right leg straight up while reaching the left hand to the right toe. Alternate sides with each step. Keep the kicking leg straight. On cadence if stationary.
