### Definition
A squat with an explosive jump at the top. Drop into a full squat, then explode upward, leaving the ground. Land softly and immediately drop into the next rep.

### How it's done
On the Q's count or OYO. Squat deep, arms back, then jump as high as possible. Land with soft knees and go right into the next squat.
