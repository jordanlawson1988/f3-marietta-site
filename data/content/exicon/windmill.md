### Definition
A standing rotational stretch. Feet wide, arms extended, rotate to touch one hand to the opposite foot while the other arm points to the sky. Opens up the hamstrings, obliques, and thoracic spine.

### How it's done
Stand with feet wider than shoulders, arms extended to the sides. Rotate and bend to touch right hand to left foot, left arm points straight up. Return to standing and alternate. On cadence.
