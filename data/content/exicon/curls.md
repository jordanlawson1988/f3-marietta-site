### Definition
A bicep curl using a coupon (cinder block, sandbag, or ruck). Arms start extended at sides, curl the coupon up to shoulder height, lower with control.

### How it's done
Stand with coupon in both hands, arms extended. Curl up to chest/shoulder height keeping elbows pinned. Lower slowly. On cadence or OYO.

### Variations
- Hammer Curls
- Wide Curls
- 21s (7 bottom half, 7 top half, 7 full)
