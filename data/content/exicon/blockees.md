### Definition
A burpee performed while holding a coupon, with an overhead press at the top. One of the most dreaded coupon exercises in F3.

### How it's done
Hold coupon at chest. Place it on the ground, jump or step feet back to plank, perform a merkin on or beside the coupon, jump feet forward, pick up coupon, stand and press it overhead. That's one. OYO.
