### Definition
A back exercise pulling a coupon from a hanging position to the chest while bent at the waist. Targets the upper back and lats.

### How it's done
Hinge at the hips with knees slightly bent, back flat. Hold coupon with both hands, arms hanging. Pull the coupon to your chest, squeeze shoulder blades, then lower. On cadence or OYO.
