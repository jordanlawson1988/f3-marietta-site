### Definition
The core F3 commitment that no man will ever be left alone regardless of fitness level.

### How it's used
If a brother is struggling, you pick up the six. We start together, we finish together. No PAX posts alone and no PAX finishes alone.

### Variations
- Leave no man behind

### Notes
This isn't just about running. It applies to life — if a man is going through it, we show up.
