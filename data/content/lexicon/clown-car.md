### Definition
When multiple PAX pile into one vehicle to ride together to a workout or event. A Clown Car is a sign of strong fellowship and often results in pre-workout mumble chatter.

### How it's done
One PAX drives and picks up others on the way to the AO, especially for Convergences, CSAUPs, or Downrange events.

### Notes
Named for the visual of too many guys climbing out of one car in a dark parking lot at 0515.
