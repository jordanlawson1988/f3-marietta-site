### Definition
The trap of a leader believing the AO exists to serve him rather than the other way around.

### How it's used
A man is Kinging when he hoards the Q, refuses to develop other leaders, or treats the AO like his personal kingdom. Avoided by rotating Q and empowering new leaders.

### Variations
- Being a King

### Notes
Kinging is the opposite of servant leadership. The cure is Living Third and giving the Q away.
