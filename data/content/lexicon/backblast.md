### Definition
A written summary of the workout posted to Slack or a website.

### How it's used
Includes the Q, the PAX, the 'Thang' (workout details), and a message from the Q.

### Variations
- Post-blast

### Notes
Keeps the Region connected and accountable.

### Related terms
- Pre-blast
