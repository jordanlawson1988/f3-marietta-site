### Definition
Leadership based on character, habit, and service. The QSource framework and the F3 way of building leaders.

### How it's used
A Virtuous Leader leads from the front, serves from the back, and builds other leaders along the way. It's not about title or authority — it's about who you are when nobody's watching.

### Variations
- Servant Leadership

### Notes
Virtuous Leadership is the output of living the QSource. It's built through daily discipline, not a weekend seminar.
