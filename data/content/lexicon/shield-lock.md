### Definition
A small accountability group of 3-4 men who meet regularly for deep trust and mutual support.

### How it's used
Named after the Spartan Phalanx formation where shields interlock. Men form Shield Locks to go deeper than the AO allows — sharing struggles, goals, and hard truths in confidence.

### Variations
- Lock Shield
- Shield Group

### Notes
A Shield Lock only works when every man is willing to be vulnerable. What's said in Shield Lock stays in Shield Lock.
