### Definition
The distance from a man's bed to the Gloom. The hardest distance to travel in any workout.

### How it's used
Win the first 43 feet and you've won the day. Once you're out of bed and moving, the rest takes care of itself.

### Variations
- Winning the 43

### Notes
Every PAX knows this truth: the hardest rep is getting your feet on the floor. Everything after that is just exercise.
