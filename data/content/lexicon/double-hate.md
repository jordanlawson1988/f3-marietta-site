### Definition
When conditions are both extreme — hot AND humid, or cold AND windy. A badge of honor to post in Double Hate.

### How it's used
"Double Hate this morning — 28 degrees with 20mph wind." Posting in Double Hate earns extra T-Claps and proves you're not controlled by comfort.

### Variations
- DH

### Notes
F3 doesn't cancel for weather. Double Hate just makes the backblast better.
