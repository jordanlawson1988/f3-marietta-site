### Definition
The intentional way F3 welcomes FNGs — approach immediately, stay with them during the workout, and make sure they have a great first experience.

### How it's used
When a new man shows up, roll out the Red Carpet. Introduce yourself, explain what's happening, stay by his side, and make sure he knows he belongs before he leaves.

### Notes
A man's first post determines whether he comes back. The Red Carpet is every PAX's responsibility, not just the Q's.
