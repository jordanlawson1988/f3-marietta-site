### Definition
Your Humble Correspondent — the PAX writing the Backblast or recap. Used in place of "I" or "me" when posting a Backblast.

### How it's used
In a Backblast, the Q or author refers to themselves as YHC rather than first person. Example: "YHC called the PAX to circle up."
