### Definition
F3 term for rain during a workout. We don't cancel for rain — we rebrand it.

### How it's used
"Looks like Liquid Sunshine for tomorrow's beatdown. See you in the Gloom." It reframes bad weather as just another reason to post.

### Variations
- LS

### Notes
If you only post when conditions are perfect, you'll miss the best beatdowns. Some of the strongest bonds are built in Liquid Sunshine.
