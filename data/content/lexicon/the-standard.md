### Definition
The commitment to post regularly — typically three or more times per week. Holding the Standard means showing up consistently regardless of weather, mood, or schedule.

### How it's used
PAX encourage each other to hold the Standard. "Are you holding the Standard this week?" A PAX who falls off is gently reminded that the Standard doesn't lower itself.
