### Definition
The philosophy that every man should be capable of leading. No single "coach" — every man eventually takes the Q.

### How it's used
F3 is not a boot camp with a hired trainer. It's a peer-led organization where leadership rotates. A leader-full AO develops men, not followers.

### Variations
- Peer-led
- Shared leadership

### Notes
If only one or two men ever Q at an AO, that AO is not leader-full — it's Kinging.
