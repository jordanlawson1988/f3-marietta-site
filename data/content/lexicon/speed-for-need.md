### Definition
The practice of faster PAX circling back to run with or encourage slower PAX. Embodies the F3 principle that no man is left behind.

### How it's done
During a run or mosey, faster PAX who reach the destination first turn around and run back toward the six, providing encouragement and keeping the group connected.
