### Definition
Completely Stupid And Utterly Pointless. A long-form or grueling event that pushes limits.

### How it's used
Events like GrowRuck, The Murph, or an Ironpax challenge that require extra endurance.

### Variations
- Goliathon
- BRR

### Notes
Meant to be hard so that men must rely on each other.
