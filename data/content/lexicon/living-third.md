### Definition
Putting others before yourself. Based on the principle of God first, others second, self third. A HIM lives for his community.

### How it's used
A man Living Third isn't asking "what's in it for me" — he's asking "who needs me today." It drives everything from Q'ing to EH to service work.

### Variations
- Third Living

### Notes
Living Third is the antidote to Kinging. When you stop serving yourself, you start leading others.
