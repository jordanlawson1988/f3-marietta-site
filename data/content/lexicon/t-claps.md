### Definition
Triple Claps. Verbal or digital praise given to a PAX for good work, effort, or achievement.

### How it's used
The F3 version of a "Like" or kudos. Drop T-Claps in Slack, at COT, or anytime a brother deserves recognition.

### Variations
- Claps
- Props

### Notes
T-Claps cost nothing and mean everything. Give them freely and often.
