### Definition
The process of getting better at life, leadership, and fitness. A man is either accelerating or decelerating — there is no standing still.

### How it's used
Accelerating means posting consistently, taking the Q, investing in your Shield Lock, serving your community, and leading your family. It's the whole man getting better across all three F's.

### Variations
- Accel
- Getting after it

### Notes
Acceleration is not perfection. It's direction. As long as you're moving forward, you're accelerating.
