### Definition
The principle of taking what you've learned and teaching the next man. F3 is free because it was given to us.

### How it's used
Once you've been sharpened by F3, you give it away — Q a beatdown, EH the Sad Clown in your neighborhood, mentor a newer PAX. The gift only works when it's passed on.

### Variations
- Pass it on
- Pay it forward

### Notes
This is why F3 will always be free. Nobody paid for what we received, and nobody should pay to receive it from us.
