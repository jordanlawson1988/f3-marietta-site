### Definition
When a man stops pushing himself and slides back into comfort. The opposite of accelerating.

### How it's used
Deceleration usually starts with fartsacking one morning, then two, then a week. Before you know it, you're a Kotter. A man is always either accelerating or decelerating — there is no neutral.
