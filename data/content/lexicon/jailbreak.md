### Definition
A free-for-all sprint or run where PAX go at their own pace rather than staying together. Often called at the end of the Thang to let faster runners open up.

### How it's done
The Q calls "Jailbreak!" and PAX run to a designated point at their own speed. No six to pick up — everyone goes all out.
