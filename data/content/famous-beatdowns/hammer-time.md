# Hammer Time

100 American Hammers, 50 LBCs, 50 Freddy Mercuries split into rounds with 30-second plank holds between each round.

## Structure
- Round 1: 25 American Hammers, 30s plank hold
- Round 2: 25 American Hammers, 30s plank hold
- Round 3: 25 American Hammers, 30s plank hold
- Round 4: 25 American Hammers, 30s plank hold
- 50 LBCs with 30s plank hold between 25-reps
- 50 Freddy Mercuries with 30s plank hold between 25-reps

## Variations
- Add side planks (15s per side) instead of regular plank holds
- Coupon Hammers — hold a coupon during all hammer reps
- Reduce to 50 Hammers, 25 LBCs, 25 Freddy Mercuries for a quicker burn

## Marietta notes
Works as a Mary-stretched-out finisher when The Battlefield Thang runs short. Can also serve as a standalone core day.
