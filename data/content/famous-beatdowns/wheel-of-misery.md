# Wheel of Misery

A randomized beatdown where reps are generated each round by rolling a die. Standard rotation of 12 exercises keeps Qs honest about variety.

## Structure
- 6–8 rounds
- Each round: roll a die twice (once for exercise, once for reps)
- Exercises: Burpees, Merkins, Squats, Big Boy Sit-ups, Hand-Release Merkins, Carolina Dry Docks, Imperial Walkers, LBCs, Mountain Climbers, Air Presses, American Hammers, Squat Jumps
- Reps: roll for 10–30 (or use a chart)
- 400m mosey between rounds

## Variations
- Bring a real die and print a paper wheel with exercise names
- Use a spinner if dice aren't available
- Add a "joker" exercise in the wheel for random chaos
- Reduce to 4 rounds for a quicker version
