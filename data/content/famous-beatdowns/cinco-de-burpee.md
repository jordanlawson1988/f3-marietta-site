# Cinco de Burpee

5 rounds of structured burpee chains with movement breaks. Total 75 burpees.

## Structure
- Round 1: 5 burpees + 50m mosey + 5 burpees + 50m mosey + 5 burpees
- Round 2: 5 burpees + 50m mosey + 5 burpees + 50m mosey + 5 burpees
- Repeat 5 times for 75 total burpees

## Variations
- Substitute burpee broad jumps for standard burpees
- Reduce to 3 rounds (45 total burpees) for FNG-friendly modifications
- Add a 100m mosey between rounds for cardio emphasis
- Partner version: one runs while the other does burpees, switch off

Sometimes Q'd on Cinco de Mayo themed weeks at The Battlefield. Works well as a shorter finisher or standalone Thang.
