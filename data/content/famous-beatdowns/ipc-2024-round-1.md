# IPC 2024 Round 1

A brutal chipper format from the Iron Pax Challenge 2024. Complete each block fully before moving to the next.

## Structure
- 100 burpees
- 100 air squats
- 100 LBCs
- 100 merkins
- For Time

## Variations
- Scale to 50% (50 reps each) for FNG-friendly modifications
- Partition into 4 rounds of 25/25/25/25 if a chipper feels too brutal
- Partner chipper: one works while the other rests
- Add a 200m mosey between each block

## Marietta notes
Ran during F3 Marietta's IPC Saturday convergences. A lung-burner that shows no mercy. Suitable for experienced Pax with strong work capacity.
