# 4 Corners

A four-station circuit where each corner of the parking lot holds one exercise. Mosey between stations for 4 rounds.

## Structure
- Corner 1: Merkins x 20
- Corner 2: Squats x 20
- Corner 3: Big Boy Sit-ups (BBSUs) x 20
- Corner 4: Burpees x 10
- Mosey between each corner (~75m per side)
- Complete 4 rounds through all corners

## Variations
- Increase reps each round (10s round 1, 20s round 2, 30s round 3, 40s round 4)
- Add a coupon at one corner for added weight
- Swap exercises each round for variety (Merkins, Squats, LBCs, Burpees)
- Reduce to 2 rounds for a quicker finisher

## Marietta notes
The four sides of the parking lot at The Battlefield work perfectly for this layout. Simple to execute and keeps the Pax moving.
