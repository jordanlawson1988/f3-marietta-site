# Lap Pyramid

Alternate a lap (or 200m mosey) with increasing reps at each end of the track, then decrease back down.

## Structure
- Round 1: 10 squats, 1 lap, 20 squats, 1 lap
- Round 2: 20 squats, 1 lap, 30 squats, 1 lap
- Continue up to 50 squats at the peak
- Then descend back down (50, 40, 30, 20, 10)
- Each lap is ~400m or 2x 200m mosey

## Variations
- Substitute Merkins or Burpees for squats
- Use a pyramid of mixed movements (first round squats, second round merkins, third round burpees)
- Cut to half reps (5, 10, 15, 20, 25 and back) for a quicker burn

## Marietta notes
The Battlefield parking lot loop is ~100m — use 2 loops as one lap. Works well as a cardio-focused Thang.
