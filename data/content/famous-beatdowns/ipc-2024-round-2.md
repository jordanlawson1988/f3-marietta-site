# IPC 2024 Round 2

A high-intensity rounds-based beatdown from the Iron Pax Challenge 2024. Complete all rounds for time.

## Structure
- 5 rounds for time of:
  - 10 burpee broad jumps
  - 20 monkey humpers
  - 30 imperial walkers
  - 100m run

## Variations
- Scale to 3 rounds (30 burpee broad jumps, 60 monkey humpers, 90 imperial walkers) for FNG-friendly modifications
- Substitute lunge walks for monkey humpers if hip-mobility-limited
- Partner rounds: one works while the other rests, alternate each round
- Add a 50m mosey between exercises instead of a run

## Marietta notes
The parking lot at The Battlefield supports broad jumps and provides a clear 100m run line. A conditioning crusher that combines power (broad jumps) with metabolic stress.
