# F3 Core Lexicon — Always-Loaded Context

This is the compressed F3 vocabulary the assistant ALWAYS knows, so that
every answer can use the right terminology without a lookup. The full
Lexicon and Exicon live in `f3Glossary.ts` and are fuzzy-matched per query.

## The 5 Principles of F3

1. **Free of charge.** No dues, no gear sales, no catch.
2. **Open to all men.** All ages, all fitness levels.
3. **Held outdoors.** Rain, shine, heat, cold — we post.
4. **Peer-led.** The Q rotates. Every PAX is expected to Q at some point.
5. **Ends in a Circle of Trust (COT).** Name-o-rama, count-off, BOM/prayer.

## The 3 Fs

- **1st F — Fitness.** The magnet. The physical workout (Beatdown) itself.
- **2nd F — Fellowship.** The brotherhood. Coffeeteria, cookouts, life.
- **3rd F — Faith.** Belief in something bigger than self. Not a religion.

## Workout Anatomy

- **Gloom** — the pre-dawn dark. When and where F3 workouts happen.
- **Muster** — the moment PAX gather at the AO for a workout.
- **Fall in** — start. Line up. The workout begins.
- **Warmup** — SSHs (Side Straddle Hops), Imperial Walkers, Don Quixotes, etc.
- **The Thang** — the main part of the Beatdown.
- **COT** — Circle of Trust. Closes every workout. Name-o-rama, count, BOM.
- **BOM** — Ball of Man. The closing prayer/reflection.
- **Name-o-rama** — each PAX says his F3 name during the COT.
- **Count-a-rama** — headcount at the start of the COT.

## Roles

- **PAX** — any man who regularly posts. Plural and singular.
- **HIM** — High Impact Man. The aspirational archetype.
- **FNG** — Friendly New Guy. First time posting. Gets a name during COT.
- **Q** — the PAX leading today's Beatdown. Peer-led, rotating.
- **Site Q** — the PAX who owns an AO week-over-week.
- **1st F Q / 2nd F Q / 3rd F Q** — regional leaders for each F.
- **Nantan** — the head leader of an F3 region.
- **Weasel Shaker** — the regional communications lead.

## Places & Artifacts

- **AO** — Area of Operation. A specific F3 workout location (e.g., The
  Battlefield, The Last Stand, The Forge).
- **Region** — a group of AOs under one leadership structure. F3 Marietta is
  a region.
- **Shovel Flag** — the symbol of an AO. Often an actual decorated shovel.
- **Coupons** — F3-speak for "weights" — cinder blocks, sandbags, rocks.
- **Ruck** — a weighted backpack workout. Also a verb (to ruck).

## Communications

- **Preblast** — announcement BEFORE a workout. Post it in Slack the day before.
- **Backblast** — writeup AFTER a workout. Records who Q'd, who posted, what
  they did. That's what the "Backblasts" page is.
- **Slackblast** — a backblast posted to Slack via the Slackblast bot.

## Key Terminology (Common PAX Questions)

- "Kotter" / "Kotters" — a PAX who returns after a long absence. Named after the TV show "Welcome Back, Kotter." Always celebrated, never shamed.
- "Sad Clown" — a man stuck in comfort and isolation, successful on the outside but dying on the inside. F3's antidote target.
- "EH" — Emotional Headlock. The friendly nudge to invite a man to F3.
- "Fartsack" — the warm bed that keeps you from posting. The enemy. "I fartsacked this morning."
- "Shield Lock" — a small group (3-4 men) who meet regularly for deep accountability. Named after the Spartan Phalanx.
- "DRP" / "Daily Red Pill" — the daily choice to get out of bed and accelerate. Inspired by The Matrix.
- "The 43 Feet" — the distance from your bed to the Gloom. The hardest distance to travel.
- "Respect" — a PAX who is 50+ years old. The PAX shout "Respect!" during Name-o-rama.
- "Downrange" — posting at an AO outside your home region while traveling.
- "T-Claps" — verbal or digital praise ("Triple Claps"). The F3 version of a Like.
- "Whetstone" — F3's mentorship program. A veteran (Vertical) mentors a newer PAX (Horizontal).
- "Convergence" — a mega workout where multiple AOs or Regions come together.
- "F3versary" — the anniversary of your first post. Usually celebrated by Q'ing.
- "VQ" — Virgin Q. A man's first time leading a workout. Milestone moment.
- "Omaha" — the Q's audible to immediately stop and switch exercises. Borrowed from Peyton Manning.
- "The Six" — the last man in the group. We pick up the Six. The group's strength is measured there.
- "The Red Carpet" — how we welcome FNGs. Approach him first, stick with him, name him.
- "Living Third" — putting others before yourself. I Am Second, but live for others (Third).

## Lexicon Essentials (Verbatim Phrases)

- "Leave no man behind, but leave no man where you found him." — the ethos.
- "Acceleration." — the goal of F3. Get better, faster.
- "Get right." — reset, recommit, post tomorrow.
- "Mumblechatter" — the running commentary/humor during a Beatdown.
- "Mary" — the core/abs portion. Named after Mary Katherine Gallagher.
- "Dora" — a partner workout pattern (e.g., "Dora 100s": 100 reps split).

## Exicon Essentials (Common Exercises)

- **Merkin** — F3 for push-up.
- **Burpee** — standard burpee. The Mount Everest of the Exicon.
- **LBC** — Little Baby Crunch.
- **Carolina Dry Dock** — shoulder-heavy push-up variant.
- **Mountain Climber** — standard mountain climber.
- **American Hammer** — seated Russian twist.
- **Squat** — standard or jump squat.
- **CDD** — Carolina Dry Dock (often abbreviated).
- **Peoples' Chair** — wall sit, arms out.
- **SSH** — Side Straddle Hop (jumping jack).
- **WMD** — Weapon of Mass Destruction — full-body nuclear exercise.
